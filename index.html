History

The mind of man plans his way, But יהוה directs his steps.
Proverbs 16:9

Steve Chab here. When I became a student of Messiah, I noticed that modern music made by Jesus-followers lacked quality and creativity in lyricism, musicality, and production.

I wondered, If God, the Author of Creation, gave artists the ability to create, then how can the music of a God-lover lack creativity?

After years of frustration, I decided to do something about it.

I partnered with the Swartz Center for Entrepreneurship (Project Olympus) at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) and Business of Music professor, Lance LaDuke. Under their guidance, I researched local and national artists and arts organizations. My wife, Elissa, and I conducted nearly 40 interviews.

What blocks artists from creating quality art?

Most churches don't foster artists, cultivate creativity, or host concerts. Many artists find that the Church doesn't understand the important role that art plays in society. One respondent said, Look to see who has more weight on society — the pastor or the artist? It's always the artist!

We learned that artists of faith in Pittsburgh need:

  • Community
  • Performance spaces
  • Professionalism

In 2021, we founded Vibrant Verse to unite, train, and showcase local artists of faith.
